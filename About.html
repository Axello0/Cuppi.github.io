Our Journey

On 18 September 2018, Axel Lo and Faye Naya Santoso, the founders of CUPPI, realized an issue where they couldnt find a comfortable alternative to single use cups whilst still maintaining a sleek design. This jumpstarted the start of CUPPI'S journey and it's still growing even now. With over 10 Million consumers and 10 new outlets!
